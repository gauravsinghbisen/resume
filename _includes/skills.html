Market Research for Indian Tourism via Indian Railways & IRCTC (Dec'13)

Research paper published in “International Conference on Research in Marketing” conducted by IIT-Delhi & Curtin University Australia.

Research validates significant increase of reach for inter-state tourism if promoted through vinyl wrapping of trains & digital marketing on IRCTC

Value Added Mobile Payment Service (Sept'13)

Research paper published in “International Journal of Multidisciplinary Research” ISSN-2277 9302.

Research explores an opportunity to pay bills using value added services of telecom companies without having a smartphone device.

Currently, technologies like Airtel Money, PayTM and all wallet companies have implemented the same.
